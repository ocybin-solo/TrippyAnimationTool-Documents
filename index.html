# TrippyAnimationToDeveloper Info: Jeffrey Box - ocybin@gmail.com **Data Collection Statement:** This application does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. **Permissions & Data Usage (Mobile Edition)** The "Trippy Animation Tool" application requires specific system permissions to function as intended. We only request access to features that directly enhance your creativity: - **Storage Access (Media/Images):** This permission allows the app to load images, and save your generated creations directly to your device's gallery or local storage. We do not access, read, or modify any other files on your device. - **Haptic Feedback (Vibration):** This permission is used to provide tactile confirmation when you interact with the UI, such as "grabbing" the scrollbar or long-pressing to delete a preset. This is for user experience only and does not collect data. - **Internet Access (Optional/Debugging):** While the core app works entirely offline, standard Android builds may include internal network permissions for basic crash reporting or system-level updates. No personal data is transmitted. **No Personal Data Collection:** Trippy Animation Tool is a "Local-First" application. We do not maintain any external servers, and we do not collect, store, or sell your name, email, location, or presets. All your presets, saved images and sprite-sheets remain strictly on your physical device unless you choose to share them manually. **Security:** I use commercially acceptable means to protect any data, even if it is just local files. ol-Documents
